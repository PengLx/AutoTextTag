Title: Time Blocking Content: Is a simple productivity exercise and means using your calendar to block time for your most important priorities.During that time, you only work on that one thing. This way, you don’t have to think, “What should I do next?”
Title: Tips for time blocking Content: Take 10 minutes every evening and plan your next day. Rearrange blocks if you must create time for other important things.Use recurring blocks for recurring tasks.Don’t over-schedule. Realistically, you can’t be productive 10 hours straight. Give yourself some time between tasks.And always schedule more time than you think you need.
