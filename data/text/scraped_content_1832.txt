Title: Meditative movement Content: Mindfulness is simply a state, a moment-to-moment awareness of one’s experience.But meditation is just the tool you use to reach that state.If you are unable to sit still, consider the meditative movement, a practice where instead of just going through the mechanical motions, you focus on the movement itself.
