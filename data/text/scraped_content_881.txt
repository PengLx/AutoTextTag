Title: Delegating = letting go Content: Delegating, or “the art of letting go”, can be one of the hardest things to grasp for a founder who enjoys having their hands in so many projects at once.While maintaining a daily role in the company is vital, it’s just as critical to make sure you’re not overworking.
Title: The art of delegation Content: Clearly define roles and responsibilities, to allow things to flow smoothly.Emphasize accountability: each personhas to understand exactly what they’re accountable for in order to grow in a way that benefits both themselves and the company.Democratize decision-making: you don't have to be in every decision-making process.Paint the big picture:understand how even the most (seemingly) menial tasks fit into the overall vision you’ve established.
