Title: A simple exercise for calm Content: It's called box breathing or four-square breathing.Here's how it works:Breathe in for four seconds.Hold the air in your lungs for four seconds.Exhale for four seconds.Hold your breath, lungs emptied, for four seconds.Box breathing improves your ability to regulate your emotions and help you feel more in control and able to deal with challenges.
