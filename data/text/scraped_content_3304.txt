Title: Why people swear Content: Swearing is used to signify a number of emotions, (e.g. anger, frustration, joy, surprise).It can be used to achieve a specific reaction from others. It can also be a useful substitute for physical violence.Swear words can also be used in a more positive manner, in the form of jokes and humor, sex talk, storytelling, self-deprecation or even social commentary.
Title: Swearing: a universal constant Content: Swearing is not just for the uneducated or people of a lower socio-economic class - it knows no social boundaries in its expression.Personality research suggests that people who swear more, score higher on traits such as extraversion, dominance, hostility and Type A personalities.
