Title: 4 Lessons From Bungee Jumping Content: Don’t overthink before deciding: On bungee jumping, the longer you wait to jump, the more you make excuses and convince yourself to back out.Live in the moment: Left hanging in mid-air while they pull you back up is an euphoric moment followed by feelings of peace and calm that you can appreciate because you are then living in the moment.Face your fears: you jump despite the fear and in the end you feel empowered and a sense of accomplishment.Have faith that things will work out: people die bungee jumping but faith the cord won’t snap keeps people jumping regardless.
