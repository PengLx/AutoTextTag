Title: The GTD system Content: The Getting Things Done system is an effective way to push through your tasks. This system defines tasks that take more than one step as projects. It is where you chart each phase of the project and list down the detailed step by step process on finishing the project. It can be a little time-consuming but it allows your brain to process in the background creating ideas and insights, instead of just storing residual tasks.
