Title: Principles of persuasion Content: Reciprocation: People will be nice if you are.Consistency:It’s easier to get people to comply with requests they see as consistent with what they’ve already said.Social proof:People will more likely say yes when they see other people doing it too. Liking:You comply with requests from people you like more than from people you don’t like.Authority: Our tendency is to be persuaded by authority figures.Scarcity:If you offer people something rare or scarce, they are more likely to want it.
