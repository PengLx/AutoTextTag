Title: Ancestral health movement Content: This movement argues that over long periods of time, evolution adapts species to their environments. It follows that when it comes to human well-being, we should pay attention to how we ate and behaved throughout the vast majority of our evolutionary history.
Title: Ancestral drives  Content: We have evolved to build strong social connections with the community through face-to-face interactions;Our brain requires regular periods of “solitude”;We have a strong drive to see their intentions manifested concretely in the world.A side effect of our current techno-culture is that it radically diminishes these ancestral drive in our daily lives.
