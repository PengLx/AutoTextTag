Title: Extreme events and happiness Content: Extreme positive and extreme negative events don't actually influence our long-term levels of happiness nearly as much as we think they would. But wehave a strong tendency to overestimate the impact that extreme events will have on our lives.
Title: The Impact Bias Content: It's present when we tend to overestimate the length or intensity of happiness that major events will create. The Impact Bias is one example of affective forecasting, which is a social psychology phenomenon that refers to our generally terrible ability as humans to predict our future emotional states.
