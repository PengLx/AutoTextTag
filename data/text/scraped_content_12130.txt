Title: Dominos infuriated Subway Content: In 2009, Domino's ad campaign focused on a blind taste test for its new oven-baked sandwiches, claiming that its sandwiches beat Subway 2-to-1.The people at Subway disagreed, and sent Domino's a cease-and-desist letter.
