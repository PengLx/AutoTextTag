Title: Nostalgia Content: Nostalgia is not the enemy of progress. Nostalgia is very motivational. People in a nostalgic mood are more optimistic about their future and inspired to pursue their goals.For instance, if you grew up fishing with your dad, you may develop a goal of having a family yourself and doing the same things with your own children.
Title: Triggering nostalgia Content: Smells, tastes, photographs, and even music can trigger nostalgia.The songs we listen to can be markers of notable events in our lives that can connect the past with the future.
