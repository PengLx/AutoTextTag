Title: Working with our hands Content: Activities that use our hands relieve stress and help solve problems:Using our hands on a task that doesn’t demand much cognitively gives the mind a chance to relax and rest.When our brain is “offline,” it gives it a chance to work on problems behind the scenes.There is something primal about it. We have actively used our hands as part of our daily survival for thousands of years.
