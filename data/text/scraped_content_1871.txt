Title: Organize your office Content: Start with a purge to create an office that is free of clutter.Create a catch-it space for incoming junk to sort through.Keep your desktop clear of clutter.Place two document trays on your desk, one for new documents, the other for opened documents that you need to deal with.Create two separate zones, one for computer work, one for non-computer work.Place physical objects into drawers or organizing trays and out of sight.Get a bigger trashcan. Make sure to recycle.
