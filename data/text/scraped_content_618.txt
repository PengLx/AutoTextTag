Title: The Ivy Lee method for peak productivity Content: At the end of the day, write the 6 most important things you need to accomplish tomorrow.Prioritize those 6 items in order of their true importance.The next day, concentrate and finish only one task at a time.At the end of the day,move any unfinished items to a new list of six tasks for the following day.Repeat this process every working day.
