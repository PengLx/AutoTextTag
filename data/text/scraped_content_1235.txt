Title: Daily prioritization check-in Content: The process of finding what is important to work on could be translated into this series of simple questions:Do I truly have to do this thing now?If the answer is Yes, is this “The Most Important Thing?”If not, where does it fit in relation to the other tasks?Is someone waiting on me for this?If so, when do they need it?Does working/not working on this now have long-term consequences that I am not aware of?
