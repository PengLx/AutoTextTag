Title: Our brains have two ways of operating Content: Focused: when you're very focused on solving the problem in front of you. But this also clamps down on unconventional and creative solutions Distracted: when you’re unfocused and your mind wanders, because you’re relaxed. This engages the autopilot area of your brain. The rest of your mind is free to wander, making connections and encouraging creative thoughts to form.
Title: Generating good ideas on command Content: Make sure you’re prepared at any time.Find out when you’re relaxed, happy and distracted (because that's when creative thoughts are more likely to appear) and make a mental note of when that’s likely to happen.Find a way to record the ideas that are sure to come.
