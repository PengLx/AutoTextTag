Title:  Content: You have to deal with people almost every day; in your job, in your social circle and even in the stores you shop in. Some people add light to your life by just being around them. On the other hand, there are people who radiate negativity. Those people threaten to destroy our peace of mind and well-being. Learn how to deal with toxic people in healthy ways and preserve your sense of happiness.
