Title: “Tell me about yourself” Content: At this point, you are just a face with a name and one of many candidates.Refrain from repeating what is on your resume or telling your life story. It might become more important to expand later if you prove to be a viable candidate.
Title: Leave an indelible mark Content: Highlight the most important aspects of your resume.Mention a passion that shows that you’re a self-starter. This is a great chance to display your quirks and interests in order to connect with the interviewer.End with why you’re here in the first place. You want the interviewer to think, “Wow, I really think this might be the one.”
