Title: Absorb and Imbibe Content: The key to opening oneself to new romantic relationships is to be completely open towards the experience. The more we are careful, cautious and afraid of things that can change us, the more we miss.Whether it is a romantic partner, a poem, a movie, a song, or a work of art, try to be a hopeless romantic, absorbing, imbibing everything. Get lost and dissolved, and don't come back unchanged.
