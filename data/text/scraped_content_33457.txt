Title: Meaning At The Workplace Content: If work is meaningful, satisfying and fulfilling, it leads to productivity and growth. The meaning of meaningful work has changed in 2020, with the health crisis along with widespread anxiety destabilizing the work culture.As the definition of meaningful work evolves, we need to ask ourselves a few questions to reflect on the significance of our work profile.
Title: Three Questions To Determine Meaning Content: Is your work creating an impact and having a multiplier effect on others, facilitating the growth of the company?Are you upgraded as compared to last quarter in terms of learning, personal growth and skills enhancement?Does your job/work profile provide you with meaningful joy?If your answer is yes to these questions, it can be a good indicator that you find true meaning in your work.
