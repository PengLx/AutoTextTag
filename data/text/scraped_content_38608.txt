Title: Habit Content: How to Develop Habits• Focuson just one habit, for 30 days.• Put it on paper, together with your motivations, obstacles, and strategies for overcoming them.• Commit fully, preferably in a public way.• Track your progress.• Remain publicly accountable — report on your progress each day.• Have support for when you falter.• Reward small wins.• If you fail, figure out what went wrong, plan for it, and try again.
