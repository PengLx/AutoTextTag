Title: Make time for exercise Content: Pick a regular (sedentary) part of your routine and switch it out for an active choice:Instead of the elevator, take the stairs;Commit to movement after the most frustrating, stressful part of your week: after a weekly meeting or work task;Work out while you watch TV: you get to indulge the part of your brain that's telling you to lie down on the couch while actually circumnavigating laziness.
