Title: Develop a taste for small talk Content: Whenever faced with uncertainty when trying to make conversation, you might want to consider making small talk.It can work wonders and it helps with getting to know the other.
Title: Learn to ask the right questions Content: Making good conversation implies mastering the technique of asking the right questions, in the correct order, in a proper tone.The purpose is to get to know the other, without making it feel like an interrogation. 
Title: Asking deep questions Content: Once you have got the chance to get to know a bit more about the other, you can initiate the use of deeper questions.These allow you to create a bond and to show to the other person how interested you are in his or her life.
