Title: Excercise against the decline of age Content: As we age, 2 forms of exercise are the most important to focus on:aerobic exercise, or cardio, which gets your heart pumping and sweat flowing;strength training, which helps keep aging muscles from dwindling over time.

Title: Tai Chi, a good practice against aging Content: The Chinese martial art combines a series of flowing movements, performed slowly and gently, with a high degree of focus and attention paid to breathing deeply.It is particularly good for older people because balance is an important component of fitness, and balance is something we lose as we get older.
