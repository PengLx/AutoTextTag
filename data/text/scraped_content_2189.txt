Title: Examples of distorted thinking Content: Seeing the world in terms of black and white extremes.Atendency to magnify our faults and minimize our achievements.Taking an isolated event and assuming that all other events will follow the same pattern.Jumping to conclusions.Catastrophising:
Title: Challenging beliefs Content: The first step is to become aware of which of these negative belief patterns you are susceptible to. Keep a journal and record your negative thoughts.Ask yourself the following questions each time you experience negative beliefs.What is my evidence for thinking this way? Is there any evidence that doesn't support this belief?Could there be other ways of interpreting this event?
