Title: Sadness and Cigarettes Content: Our moods and emotions play a powerful role in our behavioral choices and health.Extensive studies show that sadness is related to tobacco use, with people wanting to puff away more frequently while being sad.This new finding suggests smoking may not just be a habit, but deeply entwined with our emotions, and can be beneficial for future anti-smoking programs.
