
Title: Pandemic Strange ness Content: Sadly, this is becoming the new norm for many. For some this chaos has brought forth several different types of people. The ones who thought they knew who they were, others living a fantasy unknown to them, yet some know who they are, unchanged by the circumstances. This disconnectedness is nothing new, if anything the pandemic has brought forth a few awakenings for ALL people who are looking at their lifestyle and relationships more closely and how they are going to use their time wisely, how they are going to become more authentic and motivated by their reaction to this world wide pandemic. The better part is we shall learn greatly from this experience with strength and focused fortitude to a truer life well lived.
