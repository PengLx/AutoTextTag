Title: Strategic developmental feedback Content: Strategic developmental feedback requires careful thought and insightful construction, in order to help someone learn and improve. It should be:Big-picture focusedOrganizationally aligned: it should take into consideration the values of your organizationBehavioral and specific (should be clear and based on real accomplishments)Factual, not interpretiveBoth positive and negative (both positive and negative feedback work and fuel for real change)Focused on patterns, not on specific eventsLinked to impact.
