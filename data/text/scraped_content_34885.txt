Title: Sleep Experts Tricks Use When in Anxiety Content: •Make your bedroom a location for sleep only 🛏️•Wake up at the same time each day and only crawl into bed at night when you’re ready to go to sleep.🥱•Move your body a little bit each day in a way that feels good (bonus: if you do this outside).🤗•Keep to a routine of regular meals.😋•Figure out the difference between your body being sleepy and your body being tired.😪•Engage in mindless activities, like folding clothes or doing dishes, before you go to sleep.😴
