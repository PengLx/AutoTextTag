Title: The 2 goals of effective brainstorming: Content: Defer judgment (don’t get upset when people say bad ideas).Reach for quantity (come up with as many ideas as possible).
Title: Obstacles to an effective brainstorming: Content: Fear of judgment from people in positions of power;Extroverts take center stage;Groups hate scary ideas, even it they're great ones;
Title: Steps of the creative process Content: Preparation:individual study to focus your mind on the problem;Incubation:the problem enters your unconscious mind and nothing appears to be happening externally;Intimation:you get a “feeling” that a solution is on the way;Illumination:your creative idea moves to conscious awareness;Verification:your idea is consciously verified, expanded upon, and then executed.
