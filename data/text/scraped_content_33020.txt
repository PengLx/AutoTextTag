Title: Daily Motivation for everyday people Content: The point to a Daily Motivation is so you and others never feel deserted or alone in this Journey we call life. Knowledge and Experience are meant to be shared, that's why we go through them to begin with. Take 5, 10, or 15 minutes to share, love, listen to someone else that has nothing to do with your imidate personal life.Make a change and make someone else feel good in life!
