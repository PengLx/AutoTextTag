Title: Quiet people in meetings Content: What ruins business is people that don’t listen. They think they know the market but actually they don’t know anything at all.Knowing when not to talk is an art.The loudest person in the room is the dumbest because he/she doesn’t know, or have the discipline to shut up. It’s tragic. Don’t make your life a tragedy by talking too much in meetings.
