Title: Exercise as a routine, not a habit Content: Habit conjures up images of engaging in a mindless, automatic behavior, which fitness is not.Start thinking about making exercise a routine or a ritual more than a habit.According to psychologists, the difference is that routines and rituals are deliberate, purposeful, goal-oriented and mindful acts, rather than mindless ones.
Title: Practical tips Content: Physically set aside time in your schedule to be active.Pencil in workouts for three weeks so that fitness won't be "squeezed in" (or squeezed out).Find fitness buddies.Notice all of the benefits you get from working out. Itwill keep you coming back for more.
