Title: The problem with persistence Content: Failing to quit is a failure to learn. Sometimes your ideas and vision don’t match reality. What you’re trying to do isn’t going to work, staying stubbornly in the same direction can cost you much more than just pride.
Title: Deciding whether or not to give up Content: Consider these questions:Do you have an obviously better opportunity?What did you commit to? Predefine your commitment before starting, to not depend on momentary frustrations to make big decisions to quit or continue.Would your future self feel regret or relief?Do you need to give up? Or just take a break?
