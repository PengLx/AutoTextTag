Title: Reframing excuses we make for not reading: Content: Time: Stop thinking of it as some activity that you do when you feel like it. It should be a reflex, a default.Money: Reading is not a luxury. It’s a necessity.Purpose: Whatever problem you’re struggling with, is probably addressed in some book somewhere by someone a lot smarter than you.
Title: The purpose of reading Content: The purpose of reading is not just raw knowledge. Reading is part of the human experience. It helps you find meaning, understand yourself, and make your life better.
Title: Reading more Content: If you want to read more, there’s no real secret. It’s about adjusting your priorities and your perception so that reading becomes an extension of who you are and what you do.
