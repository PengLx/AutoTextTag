Title: Creativity expectations Content: Our expectations of how creativity should look like block us. Creative inspiration is all around us, but we don’t see it because we've grown up being taught to look for it in specific places.
Title: How to feel more creative Content: Take a break. Give your brain some space to think of other things.  Expand your definition of creativity. You’re probably conditioned to look for it in specific places. Break that mold.  Limit yourself. Fewer tools and less time, counterintuitively, force you to be more creative.
