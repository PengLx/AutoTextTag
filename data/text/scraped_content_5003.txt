Title: Write About Your Values Content: Writing about our personal values can make us feel loving, powerful, connected and in control while increasing our sense of power and empathy. It also makes us connect them with the events in our lives, helping reveal the deeper meanings and underlying reasons behind those events.Matching your day-to-day actions with your deepest personal values improves our ability to deal with stress.
Title: Integrity Report Content: Three-step action of working with your personal values:List out and elaborate on your core values.Conduct a self-assessment of how you have lived and worked by those core values in the past year.Hold yourself accountable for the misses and unachieved value-targets, if any.This self-assessment is essential towards improving your life and the ability to manage stressful events and circumstances.
