Title: Mastering mindful mealtimes Content: Cook or prepare food yourself if possible.Don’t scroll through social media when eating.  Turn off all streaming services.  Eat away from your desk, cubicle, or office.  Pay attention to the smell, taste, and texture of your food. Analyze the flavors and why they go well together.
Title: Intuitive eating Content: It does not approve the diet culture.It respects all body shapes and sizes.It helps you recognize your body’s cues for hunger and fullness.  It helps you evaluate habits you want to change, but without policing food.  It helps you liberate from food’s control. It makes you see food as fuel rather than filler.
