Title: 5 Tips to Feel Confident Content: Let your body do the talking. Body language and facial expressions can be just as important as your words. Your posture is key.Be mindful of your tone of voice. You make others feel at ease if your voice is calm and/or friendly.Be a good listener. How you listen is just as important as what you say. Leave any distractions alone and maintain eye contact from time to time.Take a reality check. If you find your mind going to the opposite of confident thinking, stop and check the facts.Smile. Smiling generally lightens your mood and makes other people respond more positively to you.
