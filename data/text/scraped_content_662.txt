Title: The 4 Tools of Discipline Content: Delayed Gratification: Scheduling the pain and pleasure of life in such a way as to enhance the pleasure by meeting and experiencing the pain first.Accepting Responsibility: I can solve a problem only when I say “This is my problem and it’s up to me to solve it"Dedication to Reality: The only way we can ensure our map of the world is correct and accurate is to expose it to the criticism of others.Balancing: The capacity not only to express our anger but also not to express it. Moreover, we must possess the capacity to express our anger in different ways.
