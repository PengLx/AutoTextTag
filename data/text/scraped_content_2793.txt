Title: 'Give it 5 minutes' Content: Give someone's ideas some time to set in before you start arguing against them. 
Five minutes represents “think”, not react. Because usually, the faster you react, the less you think. Not always, but often.
Title: Asking questions vs. pushing back Content: Pushing back means you already think you know.Asking questions means you want to know. Ask more questions.
