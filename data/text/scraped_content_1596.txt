Title: The Spotlight Effect Content: It refers to our tendency to overestimate how much other people notice about us: we tend to think there is a spotlight on us, highlighting all of our mistakes or flaws, for all the world to see.The spotlight effect is the resultof egocentrism: we seeour entire existence from our own experiences and perspective.And we use those experiences to evaluate the world around us, including other people.
