Title: Happiness my way. Content: I have always been dwelling on keeping myself happy. Getting good grades, finding true love, having more money. I thought these things would make me smile. I tried hard on being happy that I no longer felt happier. I was a lost soul without emotion.Happiness came when I was grateful for others, when I was honest with myself, when I did the things I loved. Changing my perspective completely has made me more happier. Don't chase for materialistic stuffs and find happiness in little things we do.
