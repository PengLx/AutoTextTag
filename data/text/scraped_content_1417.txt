Title: Ways to Say No Content: The Indirect “No."Say no without feeling uncomfortable by explaining the reasons why you can't.The “Let me get back to you.”Buy yourself time to think if you could do it and come up of a way to say no.The Conditional “Yes.”With this conditional yes, we force people to prioritize.
