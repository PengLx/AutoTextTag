Title: Principles for giving effective feedback Content: Make it relevant: if what you're telling someone isn't aligned with their goals in some way, the impact of your feedback will be limited. Stay focused on a limited number of issues you want to address.Provide context, if you hope to influence someone's behavior.Listen carefully.Be compassionate.Follow-up: from time to time, refer back to what you agreed when you had your conversation.
