Title: High-intensity interval training (HIIT) Content: Any exercise can be a form of HIIT, if it follows a specific regimen where you vary your speeds and intensity when doing it.Example: Jogging lightly for three minutes.Pushing yourself harder for a minute (run or sprint).Repeating this cycle 4 more times.
Title: HIIT home workout Content: HIIT workouts generally only last about 20 minutes, which makes them attractive for busy people (most of us).A  20-Min HIIT home workout:Dynamic Warm-upBodyweight SquatsPush-upsStar Jacks (or other dynamic exercises)Inverted Row (or other pull exercises)Plank (or other core exercises).A  stretching to cool down.
