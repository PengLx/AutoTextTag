Title: Pomodoro Technique Content: “You learn with your full attention for 25 minutes and take a 5 minute break.” Every 25 minute period is called Pomodoro!According to Primacy,You remember more the beginning of anything you have learned.In Recency,You remember end things you have learned.For example,If you went to a party and met 30 strangers.You are more likely to remember the first few and last ones!So whenever you read a book,Use this technique.As there shall be more primacy and recency,You will remember more!
