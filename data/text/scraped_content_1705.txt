Title: 5 dangers of criticism Content: Losing yourself.Isolation.Self-justification.Bitterness.Revenge.
Title: Transforming criticism Content: Criticism, even done poorly, transforms leaders. The sting of criticism given years ago can still cling years later. Most often, criticisms humble you.
Title: Preventing criticism Content: Engage peopleearly and often. Avoid isolation. It’s difficultto criticize the plan you had a hand in making.Choose teammates carefully. Don’t give persistent critics a seat at the table.
Title: Inviting criticism Content: Criticism is desirable and useful, even if it stings.Take criticism to a useful place by asking:What do you suggest?How can I be better?What do you want?"Thank you," points criticism toward useful ends.
