Title: How to embrace an uncertain future Content: Replace expectations with plans. You can guide your tomorrow, but you can’t control the exact outcome.Prepare for different possibilities.Become a feeling observer.It isn’t the uncertainty that usually bothers you; it’s the tendency to get lost in your feelings about it.Get confident about your coping and adapting skills.Ask yourself, “What’s the worst that can happen?”Practice mindfulness. Notice and appreciate the beauty of the moment.
