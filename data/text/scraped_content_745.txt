Title: Addressing reluctance Content: The problem of reluctance is commitment, not negativity.The worst thing you can do is explain why it-will-work to people who aren’t committed to make-it-work.
Title: Help reluctant people make commitments Content: Listen to constructive dissent. Don’t minimize concerns.Keep the big picture in focus. Describe success and tell stories of past success.Create safety nets. Make commitment less dangerous.Make commitments. Reluctant teams are often led by leadership that’s playing it safe.Build relationships. Strong connection enables deep commitment.Divide responsibility between several people.
