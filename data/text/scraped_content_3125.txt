Title: Mindfulness meditation Content: It is based on Buddhist traditions and it's described as "the non-judgmental awareness of experiences in the present moment."Sit in a quiet place with your eyes closed, focusing on your breath as it moves in and out.It's a way to become familiar with your own mind.
Title: How meditation works Content: It works through a combination of several distinct mechanisms:Attention regulation. Focused attention for an extended period of time.Body awareness. Paying attention to surroundings, thoughts and bodily sensations.Emotion regulation. Learning to observe your thoughts and accept them without reactive judgment while refraining from the habitual response.Change in perspective on the self.
