Title: Reasons for serious lies Content: To avoid punishment. These lies are told when there is a threat of loss if the lie is uncovered.Concealing a benefit. These lies are told to conceal the reward obtained by breaching a law or expectation.Protecting yourself or someone else from serious harm.
