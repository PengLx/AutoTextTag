Title: Design your life Content: When you're faced with a challenge and need to make a decision, your brain starts to look for the path of least resistance. Frequently, you go with the default option.You just designed your life as a choice architect .On one hand, you encourage smarter decisions you want to do by making them more accessible. And you add friction to habits you want to quit , making them less accessible, or remove the option to perform them altogether.
