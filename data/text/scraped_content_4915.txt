Personal Development,Time Management,Productivity
Title: Setting work goals Content: Set goals at regular intervals and give yourself enough time. When you think you might not meet a goal, take a step back to asses the situation.Be transparent: let your manager and team know as soon as you've slid off-track. They're there to help.Be kind to yourself. If you fail, give yourself some grace.
Title: Goals should be aspirational Content: If you fail to meet them, you probably set difficult ones for yourself.Goals should inspire you to stretch yourself. If you hit all of your goals every time, they might not be ambitious enough.
